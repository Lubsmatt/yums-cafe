Our Story

A taste of everywhere, in one Ntinda garden.

Tucked along Kiwatule Road next to St. Francis Primary School, Yums Café brings together Continental, Italian, Chinese, Thai, Asian and char-grilled local flavours under one roof — with a touch of Local Twist.

Whether it's a lunch break, a long catch-up with friends, or a private celebration, our calm ambience, soft music and open-air lounge make every table feel like home.
